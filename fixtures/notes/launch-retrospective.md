# Pilot Launch Retrospective

**Date:** 18 July 2026  
**Facilitator:** Jordan Lee

## Timeline

The pilot launch moved from 8 July to 15 July because the external document-processing vendor's security review took one week longer than planned. The application itself passed QA on schedule, so the team did not cut any product scope.

During the delay, the team held a short risk stand-up at 10:00 AM each weekday. That meeting ends now that the pilot is live; the usual Tuesday delivery sync remains.

## What went well

The staged rollout caught a permissions problem before it reached all pilot customers. Support's launch checklist gave engineering a clear escalation path, and all five pilot customers completed onboarding.

## Improvement

Future plans should make vendor review lead times explicit. Jordan will add a vendor-readiness checkpoint four weeks before the next launch.
